What's In My Food?

How does What's In My Food work?

WIMF accesses a public database full of thousands of food product barcodes. Once you type in a barcode, it scans the database for a product match and returns nutritional info such as common allergens, fats, carbs, and sugars, whether it's vegan or not, and if the product contains palm oil.


Why is my product not found?

Open Food Facts is a collaborative project made by people in the community. If a product barcode you entered is not found and you want to help contribute, just visit world.openfoodfacts.org to add your product info. This helps the next person out!
